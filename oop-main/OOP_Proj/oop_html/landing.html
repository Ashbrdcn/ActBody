Welcome to Activebody

Fitness should be accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

At Active Body, our home workout programs are tailored to suit all levels, helping you achieve your fitness goals with convenience and expert guidance.

Beginner

Simple bodyweight exercises like squats and push-ups, done 3-4 times a week to build basic strength and endurance.

Intermediate

Add weights and more intense exercises like burpees and dumbbell squats, 4-5 times a week.

Advanced

High-intensity workouts (HIIT) and heavier weights, done 5-6 times a week.
